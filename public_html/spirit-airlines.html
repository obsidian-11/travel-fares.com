Q. How can I check-in and get my flight boarding pass?

Ans. Check-in for flights begins 24 hours before departure and ends an hour before departure. Passengers can check-in in three ways viz.

  • Online Check-In: To check in online, visit the official website, click on the 'Check In' tab, and follow the further instructions.
  • Airport Check-In: Self-service kiosks are available at the airport or you can contact customer service agents at the airport.
Q. Can I purchase a separate seat for my infant?

Ans.Yes, you can make a separate seat reservation for an infant; however, the infant must be securely placed in an FAA (Federal Aviation Administration)-approved child restraint system (car seat).

You can also view the child restraint system guidelines that the FAA has approved for use on aircraft along with the safety and security procedures used at the airport for infants and children.

Q. How much does Spirit Airlines charge for seat assignments?

Q. Can I check-in online for my spirit airlines flight?
