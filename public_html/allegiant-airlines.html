Allegiant Air (abbreviated Allegiant) is a low-cost airline based in the United States which conducts both planned and charter planes. It is the fourteenth-biggest commercial airline in Northern America as the main air carrier.

It was established in 1997 as WestJet Express and is fully controlled by Allegiant Travel Agency, an officially traded firm with 4,000 staff and a market capitalization of more than US$2.6 billion. Summerlin, Nevada, a Las Vegas suburb, houses the corporate head offices. Allegiant Air was established in January 1997 as WestJet Express by Mitch Allee (founder, CEO), Jim Patterson (governor), and Dave Beadle (chief pilot). After losing a logo issue with West Jet Air Center of South Dakota, Rapid City, and identifying the title's resemblance to WestJet Airlines of Alberta, Calgary, the airline changed its title to Allegiant Air and obtained FAA and US DOT accreditation for planned and domestic charter flights on June 19, 1998.

Allegiant Airlines Flight Cancelation Policy

Sometimes, things may not go according to our will and we have to cancel our plans, right? The same is with air travel. If you are having a change in your air travel plans, Allegiant Airlines comes forward for an easy and hassle-free ticket cancelation policy.

  • Allegiant 24 hours Cancellation Policy: You can cancel a flight ticket within 24 hours and get a full refund only if you have booked your flight at least 7 days prior to departure. Allegiant's 24- hour flight cancellation policy applies to all types of tickets.
  • Allegiant Cancellation Policy for Standard Tickets: If you are canceling your allegiant airline flight ticket after 24 hours of booking, you will be charged a cancellation fee of $75 per passenger per segment. You will be issued a non-refundable future credit after a deduction of $75 from the original amount you paid.
  • Allegiant Cancellation Policy on Award Tickets: If you want to cancel an award ticket due to a sudden change in your travel plan, then you will be charged $75 as the cancellation fee. Please note that cancellation charges for Allegiant Airlines Flights in both cases (Standard and Award) are the same.

Key Points of Allegiant Airlines Cancellation Policy

To initiate the process of flight cancellation with Allegiant Airlines, you'll need to be well-versed with the cancellation policy to avoid last-minute cancellation trouble. So, to make the cancellation process easier, please refer to the following points as mentioned below:

  1. For the tickets canceled within 24 hours of the original purchase, the full amount of the airfare will be credited to the customer's original account of payment and should be done within a week from the date of departure.
  2. Passengers who are canceling their Allegiant tickets 24 hours can still cancel their booking but they need to pay a cancellation fee.
  3. The Allegiant flight cancelation fee depends upon various factors including the travel date, the number of canceling tickets, etc.
  4. To cancel a flight online, passengers can proceed through the "Manage Trip" option on the homepage of the official website.
  5. Customers who are eligible for a refund will be offered the refund within 7-8 business days from the date of the ticket cancellation.

Cancellation of Standard Tickets

You can cancel your standard Allegiant Airlines flight ticket under the 24 hours free cancellation window. However, if you cancel it after 24 hours of the ticket purchase, you have to pay $75 per segment per passenger as the flight cancellation charge. The amount will be deducted from the original amount paid. The remaining balance will be converted into a travel credit that you can use to book an Allegiant ticket in the future.

Cancellation of Award Tickets

The cancellation of award tickets follows the same policy as the cancellation of standard tickets. You can cancel your award tickets for free within the 24 hours cancellation window, but you will be charged $75 from the original amount you paid if you cancel your ticket after the closure of 24 hours window.

Cancellation due to Unpleasant Weather

Allegiant Airlines keeps the safety of its passengers as the utmost priority, and so it has established a cancellation policy due to bad weather. You can exercise any three of these options in that case:

  • Travel on the rescheduled flight
  • Rebook your flight on a different date without getting charged with a fee
  • Cancel your Allegiant flight ticket and ask for a refund

You can make changes to your flight by selecting the "Manage Travel" option on the allegiantair.com website.

Canceled Allegiant Flight

The Allegiant canceled flight policy applies to those flights canceled by the airline itself. In that case, you will have two options to act upon:

  • You can change your travel dates for the same cities without any fee or fare difference (online via Manage Travel).
  • Cancel your flight(s) for a refund.

Your itinerary will be canceled automatically within 72 hours if you didn't cancel your ticket, and an Allegiant travel credit voucher will be issued for the full value of the ticket for your future travel, or you get the refund via customer care.
