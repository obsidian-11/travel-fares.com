Alaska Airlines Baggage Allowance

Book a Flight Alaska Airlines, You will charge $30 to confirm your first backpack and $40 to secure your second backpack for luggage weighing up to 50lbs and measuring 62" (L+W+H) or less throughout digital check or at the airport.

When you confirm extra bags on Alaska Airlines, you will be charged $75 for your 3rd checked luggage and $75 for each checked baggage after that.

Most luggage with a total length, width, and height of 63 to 115 inches will be considered overstuffed. Any item with an absolute measurement of more than 115 inches will not be approved. Alaska Airlines charges overly large backpack charges in addition to any standard, excess, or overloaded charges.

Alaska Airlines Pet Policy

Traveling with pets is always a challenging task, especially if it is a flight journey. Also, with the rules and regulations on the air travel of animals, it becomes more confusing to book pets on board. In that case, Alaska Airlines is a trusted and pet-friendly air travel partner. Alaska Airlines pet policy keeps it simple and easy for the pet travel on its flights. Customers can book their pets in advance to ensure there is a space for them on the flight. The airlines allow them to take small dogs and others pets such as cats, rabbits, etc., on board the flight.

Pet Carrier Requirements
  • The carrier must be of solid construction and composed of plastic, wood, or metal and able to withstand the rigors of normal transportation.
  • The Pet carrier must be secured with nut- and bolt-type fasteners if it is not constructed as one piece.
  • The carrier must be comfortable and allow an easy movement of the pet. The pet must be able to lie down and turn around in a normal, comfortable position.
  • The carrier must be clean and leak-proof with absorbent bedding made of safe and non-toxic material.
  • It must not have sharp points or edges, no protrusions with the potential to injure the animal.
  • The carrier must have adequate air ventilation and vents must be covered with bars or any other sturdy material.
  • It must have handles on the exterior to lift without tipping.
Pet Carrier Measurements
  • Extra Small Pet Carrier of 100 size dimensions - 15" x 16" x 21"
  • Small Pet Carrier of 200 size dimensions - 19" x 20" x 27"
  • Medium Pet Carrier of 300 size dimensions - 23" x 22" x 32"
  • Large Pet Carrier of 400 size dimensions - 26" X 24" x 36"
  • Extra Large Pet Carrier of 500 size dimensions - 30" x 27" x 40"
Please note that:
  1. Extra-large pet carriers (Size 500) will be accepted on Boeing 737 aircraft, Alaska Airlines flight series 001-999, and some 2000 series flights operated with Bombardier Q400 aircraft. To know more about pet policy, customers can reach out to the Alaska Airlines reservations helpdesk.
  2. Pets will not be allowed to travel in the baggage cabin of the Airbus airplane. Contact reservations helpdesk to confirm the pet accommodation.
