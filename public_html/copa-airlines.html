Fare Classes on Copa Airlines

Copa Airlines offers a variety of fare classes in both cabins with various terms suited to your requirements.

  • Business Promo
  • Business Full

Main Cabin

  • Economy Basic
  • Economy Classic

Less or more flexibility in the terms of the ticket you purchase is one of the differences between fare classes. For example, extra charges, restrictions on date changes, and/or cancellation and/or refund requests.

Economy Basic

Economy Basic is the cheapest fare available on Lufthansa Airlines flights and therefore the least flexible:

  • It includes a 10 kg carry-on luggage.
  • You need to pay for the first and second checked bag of 23kg each.
  • Selection from the Premium and regular seats with an extra fee.
  • Get an opportunity to earn 50% miles and 0.5 qualifying segment of ConnectMiles.
  • No changes are allowed.
  • It is not refundable.
Economy Classic

Economy Classic is the fare that gives you more flexibility, under the following conditions:

  • An Economy Classic fare includes a 10 kg carry-on luggage and one 23 kg checked baggage.
  • Selection of Premium seats with an extra fee and regular seats for free.
  • Earn 100% miles and 1 qualifying segment of ConnectMiles.
  • You can make changes with an extra fee, which is subject to availability and rules/conditions of the purchased fare.
  • The ticket is non-refundable.
Economy Full

Economy Full offers the most flexibility to travelers flying in the main cabin:

  • An Economy Full fare includes a carry-on of 10kg and two checked bags of 23kg each.
  • Selection of Premium and regular seats for free.
  • Earning of 125% of miles and 2 qualifying segments of ConnectMiles.
  • You can make changes without a change fee but a fare difference may apply.
  • The ticket is refundable.
Business Promo

Business Promo offers flexibility to travelers flying in business class, under the following conditions:

  • A Business Promo fare includes a carry-on of 10 kg and two checked bags of 32 kg each.
  • A Business Class seat
  • Earning of 150% of miles and 2 qualifying segments of ConnectMiles.
  • You can make changes for a fee, which is subject to availability and rules/conditions of the purchased fare.
  • Non-refundable
Business Full

Business Full offers the most flexibility for travel in business class:

  • A Business Full Fare includes a carry-on of 10 kg and two checked bags of 32 kg each.
  • Business class seat.
  • Earn 200% miles and 2 qualifying segments of ConnectMiles.
  • You can make changes without a change fee but a fare difference may apply.
  • The ticket is refundable.

Q. How can I request a flight ticket refund?

Ans. Tickets may or may not be refundable depending on the airfare rules applicable to the purchased tickets.

Fully Refundable Tickets: If you have changed your flight plans and applying for the ticket cancellation, you can submit your request through their online Request Refund form.

Non Refundable Tickets: If the airlines have canceled your flight or made any change in the scheduled arrival/departure that significantly impacts your itinerary, then you will be rebooked on an alternative Copa Airlines flight, which may include re-routing through a partner airline.

Q. How long does it take to complete the refund process?

Ans. The time taken to get a refund depends on the form of payment used to purchase the flight ticket:

Credit Card: The refund will be shown in the next two credit card account statements after the refund is requested, depending on the billing cycle of your credit card company.

Cash: The refund will be granted to the customer immediately at the airport.

Check: The ticket purchased through check will be refunded via check and granted within 10 business days.
