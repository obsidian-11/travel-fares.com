How to make Frontier Airlines Reservations Online

You can make flight reservations through several methods: by using the official website, mobile application, or directly calling the Frontier helpdesk. To book Frontier flight tickets online, follow these steps and make smooth flight reservations:

  1. Open www.flyfrontier.com in your device browser and find the flight search engine.
  2. Click on the "BOOK A FLIGHT" tab and choose your trip type from three options, viz., Round-trip or One-way.
  3. Fill in the "From" (source airport) and "To" (destination airport) in the search box.
  4. Select the travel date of your departure from the "Depart Date" option.
  5. Also, select the "Return Date" if you have selected the round trip.
  6. Next, enter the total number of travelers (such as Adults, Lap Infants, & Children) in the "Travelers" section.
  7. Enter the "Promo Code" (if any). Then, click on the search button.
  8. Select the suitable flight as per your travel plans from a long list of flights available for that itinerary. Now, enter passengers' details such as name, age, gender, address, contact details, etc.
  9. Further, follow all the instructions on the website carefully and fill in all the details correctly.
  10. Finally, make your payments and finish the Frontier flight reservation process.
  11. You will receive a booking/confirmation number. Keep it safe for future purposes.

Travel with Luggage on Frontier Airlines Flights

Frontier Airlines is one of those airlines that lets a passenger to carry a small "Personal Item" without any charge but charge a fee for the carry-on bag and standard checked baggage. It means, you need to be prepared for every checked bag as well.

Personal Item
  • The price of a personal item will be included in the Frontier Airlines booking.
  • The maximum dimensions of the personal item must be no larger than 14"H X 18"W X 8"D.
  • A personal item includes purses, briefcases, laptop bags, totes, diaper bags and kids backpacks.
Carry-on Baggage
  • You need to pay for a carry-on bag onboard a Frontier flight.
  • The maximum dimensions of the carry-on must be no larger than 24"H X 16"W X 10"D and < 35lbs.
  • A carry-on baggage includes large small duffel bags, backpacks, and small suitcases/wheeled bags.
  • The price of a carry-on bag is included with the WORKS bundle of Frontier Airlines.
Checked Baggage
  • You can purchase a checked bag up to 45 minutes before domestic and 60 minutes before international flights.
  • The maximum dimensions of a checked baggage must be no larger than 62 linear inches and < 40lbs.
  • A checked baggage includes large duffel bags, large suitcases, large musical instruments, and sporting equipment.
Maximum weight - changes for Frontier flight bookings effective January 18, 2022; overweight bags subject to charge
  • Maximum 50 pounds (22.6 kilograms) for tickets purchased before January 18, 2022
  • Maximum 50 pounds (22.6 kilograms) for tickets purchased on or after January 18, 2022 and travel before March 1, 2022
  • Maximum 40 pounds (18.1 kilograms) for tickets purchased on or after January 18, 2022 and travel on or after March 1, 2022

Please note that No baggage exceeding 110 linear inches or weighing more than 100 pounds (45 kilogram) will be accepted, with the exception of assistance devices and musical instruments.

Frontier Airlines Baggage Allowance

While each customer is permitted one free private item, Frontier charges fees for all carry-on backpack. Carry-on backpacks must be no larger than 10" x 16" x 24" and no heavier than 35lbs. Luggage larger than this size should be confirmed at the gate for a fee of $60.

Frontier charges luggage fees for backpacks weighing up to 50 pounds and measuring 62 inches (L+W+H) or less for the first and second verified luggage. Checked luggage fees range from $30 to $55, based on whether they are remunerated in advance or at the airport.

Extra luggage defined as three or more backpacks per traveller, will charge $85-$95 per backpack on Frontier Airlines planes.

Overloaded baggage is defined as any backpack weighing more than 50 pounds. Frontier charges fees for overloaded baggage in relation to any standard, excess, or over large luggage fees. Backpacks weighing more than 99 pounds are not permitted as verified baggage on Frontier.

Most luggage with a length, width, and height total of more than 62 inches are considered overloaded. Frontier charges over large luggage charges in relation to any standard, excess, or overloaded fees. Bags with total measurements bigger than 109" will not be approved as verified baggage.

Frontier Airlines Check-In Policy

Frontier Airlines offers multiple options to check in for their Frontier flights. Several options include online check-in, mobile app check-in, airport check-in, and priority check-in. Have a look at the various Frontier flights check-in methods.

Online Check-In: Passengers can check-in for their flights through the official website. By visiting flyfrontier.com, they can check-in up to 24 hours prior to the scheduled flight departure (until 60 minutes prior to departure). They can view their entire itinerary, add or purchase bags/seats, and print Frontier flight boarding pass. If you are purchasing bags at the airport, remember bags cost more at the airport.

Mobile Check-In: by downloading the official Frontier mobile application, customers can easily check-in and get their mobile boarding pass. They can skip the airport line if they have their mobile boarding pass in hand and are not checking a bag or just have a carry-on. Just head straight to the gate! The free Frontier mobile app is available on iOS and Android.

Airport Check-In: Frontier Airlines also gives the facility to customers who won't get their check-in done online or through the mobile application. There is self-service kiosks check-in at many airports. Simply enter the flight confirmation code, confirmation email, credit card, and ID, or passport details. Passengers can quickly view their entire itinerary, purchase checked or carry-on bags, upgrade seating, and print their boarding pass. Full-service Frontier check-in counters are available at all airports.

Priority Check-In: If a customer possesses or purchased one of Frontier's bundles, purchased a carry-on bag, or if he/she is a FRONTIER Miles Elite member, they can undergo the Priority Check- In at their ticket counters.

Frontier Airlines Check-in Times

It is recommended that the passengers must arrive at least 2 hours before the scheduled flight departure time. You must be in possession of your printed boarding pass and any checked baggage by the cutoff times:

  • International Frontier flights- 60 minutes prior to the scheduled departure time
  • Domestic Frontier flights- 45 minutes prior to the scheduled departure time

These check-in timings are set for your comfort and convenience, and it is advised to every traveler to arrive no later than 30 minutes before scheduled flight departure.

Frontier Airlines FAQs

Q. How do I check-in for my Frontier Airlines Flight?

Ans. You can check-in for your flight via several methods: Online, Mobile, and Airport check-in.

  • Online Check-in: Visit 'Online Check in' page of Frontier Airlines official website and check-in up to 24 hours prior to flight departure (until 60 minutes prior to departure).
  • Mobile Check-in: Open the Frontier airlines official application for iOS and Android to check in and get the boarding pass with ease.
  • Airport Check-in: You can get your check-in for your flight and get your boarding pass at Frontier's self-service kiosks. Simply bring a confirmation code, confirmation email, credit card, and ID, or passport.
Q. How to manage my flights with Frontier Airlines?

Ans. To manage your flights or reservations, visit the official website of Frontier airlines or open the mobile application of the airline; Go to the 'My Trip' section or 'My Flights' section appearing on the app to retrieve your Frontier Airlines booking.

Q. How much is the fees for checked baggage on Frontier Airlines?

Ans. Frontier Airlines charges baggage fees for most flights flying within the United States. The checked baggage fee may vary based on fare class, frequent flier status, and specific route flown.

  • First checked bag: $30-$50 per bag costs up to 50 lbs.
  • Second checked bag: $45-$55per bag cost up to 50 lbs.
  • Third checked bag: $85-$95 per bag costs up to 50 lbs.
  • Overweight bag (51-99lbs): +$75 per bag cost
  • Oversized bag (63"-109"): +$75 per bag cost
Q. How to use a flight credit or e-ticket?

Ans. To use flight credit from an unflown flight, simply book your new frontier airlines flight from the official website. Once you reach the payment screen, select "Credit with Frontier" and enter the 6-digit confirmation code (from the original booking) for the credit.

Q. What important things to keep in mind while using flight credit or e- ticket?

Ans.

  • Credits cannot be redeemed to cash.
  • Credits from a reservation purchased expire in one (1) year from the date of issue.
  • Credits may not cover the entire value of the purchase. The remaining balance must be paid by the customer with a credit card.
  • Credits are non-transferable.
  • The name on the new reservation using the credit must match the old reservation.
Q. How do I add bags or seats to my Frontier Airlines Reservations?

Ans.

If you already purchased a ticket:

Navigate to 'Manage Booking' in the navigation menu on its official website. Then, select 'My Trip' and enter your Last Name and Confirmation Code.

If you want to purchase bags/seats while Check-In:

You can add seats and bags while checking in online within 24 hours of departure. Open 'Manage Travel' on the top of the website and select Online Check-In. Enter your Last Name and Confirmation Code. You can check-in for your frontier flight, and add bags/seats at the same time while being online.

Q. Can I bring a stroller onboard the Frontier Airlines flight?

Ans. Yes, you can bring a stroller onboard Frontier flights. It must be checked at the gate after you arrive at the airport. It is checked in free of charge.

Q. How do I check my flight status?

Ans. Visit 'Flight Status' to view the details of your frontier flight.

For airport or terminal information, visit the airport terminal information tab on the left of the official website or visit its Airport Information page.
