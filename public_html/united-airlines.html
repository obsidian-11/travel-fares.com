United Airlines Check-In Policy

Book a flight United Airlines and get the exclusive services on board the flights. United offers several options to check-in for the flight seamlessly and comfortably. Customers can use Online Check-in, Mobile Check-in, Kiosk Check-in, Auto Check-in, and Curbside Check-in options.

Online Check-In:

Visit the official website of United Airlines and click on the 'Check In' option under the 'My Trips' tab. You can also check in through this link: www.united.com/checkin.

Mobile Check-In:

Open the United Airlines mobile application and check-in directly through the smartphone.

Kiosk Check-In:

Visit the United Airlines self-serve kiosk at the airport and check-in by MileagePlus card, passport, or driver's license. You need to scan your passport or boarding document and enter the e-Ticket number or confirmation number. The kiosk can also print a flight boarding pass, flight change, seat upgrade, and viewing seat maps.

Auto Check-in:

This option enables customers to check-in online for the remaining segment of their itinerary and can also be done at airport self-service kiosks or through the mobile application.

Curbside Check-in:

This check-in service is offered by several airports across the United States. In this way, you need to provide the e-Ticket number or photo ID, United Airlines flight reservation code, and flight destination.

Please note:

  • For United and United Express-operated flights within the U.S., passengers without checked bags must check-in at least 30 minutes before departure.
  • For United and United Express-operated flights within the U.S., Passengers with checked bags must check-in at least 45 minutes before the flight departure.
  • For United and United Express-operated international flights, Passengers must check-in at least 60 minutes before flight departure, no matter whether they are checking baggage or not.

Pet Travel with United Airlines

United Airlines is a pet-friendly air passenger carrier giant that offers pet travel onboard its flights. United ensures a quality, safe and comfortable experience for a pet traveling with its owner. It knows the significance of a furry animal friend in a human's life, and that's why United has come up with a well-defined pet policy. The policy has some important points regarding pet travel, which goes according to the rules and guidelines by the International Pet and Animal Transportation Association (IPATA). United Airlines cheap flight reservations make the booking easier and hassle-free.

In-Cabin Pets

United Allows domesticated cats and dogs on board its flights that can fit in a pet carrier underneath the seat in front of the passenger. These are called in-cabin pets.

Pets can't be in-cabin for flights to/from or through Hawaii and some international cities, but they are allowed on select United Airlines flights.

Passengers can bring a pet kennel aboard in addition to a carry-on bag allowance. There is a $125 service charge each way with an additional $125 service charge for each stopover of more than 4 hours within the United States or more than 24 hours outside the U.S. United Airlines reservations airline tickets are available on the website at affordable rates.

Requirements for In-Cabin Pets
  • Puppies and kittens must be older than two months for domestic United flights, while they must be at least four months older for international flights due to rabies vaccination requirements.
  • Pets aren't allowed to travel with kids and unaccompanied minors.
  • There will be an extra ticket of $125 each way for an additional pet. The additional pet must also remain in the floor space under the seat with another pet.
  • If a pet doesn't fit in a kennel, they'll need to travel with United's PetSafe Program.
  • Pets must travel on the same flight as the owner's.
  • Oxygen service won't be available for pets in case of an emergency.
Pet Carriers and Kennel Guidelines
  • The pet must be carried in an approved hard-sided or softy-sided carrier.
  • The carrier must fit completely under the seat in front of the passenger and remain there every time.
  • The hard-sided pet carrier must be 17.5" long x 12" wide x 7.5" high (44 cm x 30 cm x 19 cm).
  • The soft-sided carrier must be 18" long x 11" wide x 11" high (46 cm x 28 cm x 28 cm).
  • The soft-sided pet carriers can exceed the specified dimensions because they are collapsible and easily fit underneath a seat without blocking the aisle.
  • Only one pet is allowed per carrier, and the animal must be able to sit and turn around comfortably.

United Airlines Flight Cancellation & Refund Policy

Do you want to cancel your United Airlines flight because your travel plans have changed? Or are you dropping your United flight journey plan dues to some unprecedented reasons? Well, here is the United Airlines flight cancellation policy that you need to know to get your flights canceled hassle-free, and so you save some bucks while doing that. This policy allows you to cancel a United flight ticket in a hassle-free manner and without any delay. It can save your time and money if you end up with the need to cancel. United Airlines offers you to change or cancel your flight reservation.

  • If you cancel your United Airlines booking online within 24 hours of the ticket purchase, you won't have to pay any flight cancellation charges.
  • If you are canceling your United ticket after the passage of 24 hours of the ticket purchase, you will be charged some nominal charges according to your travel class.
  • If you have purchased a refundable United ticket, you can cancel your flight within seven days of the scheduled flight take-off.
  • If you have canceled your United flight ticket on the day of departure, your total ticket fare will be fortified. You will not receive a refund in this case.
  • If you have made your United Airlines flight booking from a third-party website, you need to acknowledge the cancellation policy of that website regarding the refund.
You will be entitled to get a refund if:
  • Your flight departing from the European Union, Norway, Iceland, or Switzerland is:
    • Delayed by 5 hours or more, you can cancel your flight ticket without penalty and request a refund (plus compensation).
    • Delayed by 3 hours or more, you are entitled to receive compensation if the flight delay was within the control of United Airlines.
  • All types of flight tickets are eligible for cancellations and refunds, such as in case of death, severe or chronic illness, and jury duty, including tickets purchased by using cash/cheque/credit/debit card, award tickets, as well as bulk fares, and net fares.
  • United's Basic Economy tickets cannot be canceled but qualify for a full refund within the 24-hour window if you have purchased the ticket a week or more before the scheduled departure date.
  • The estimated processing time for refunds from credit card purchases is seven business days. Other modes of refunds are processed within 20 business days as usual.
  • If you want a refund on a ticket issued through a travel agency or a third-party website, you need to contact the agency directly.

United Airlines FAQs

Q. Can I book flights for multiple-stop itineraries online with United Airlines?

Ans. Yes, you can buy United flight tickets for multiple-destination travel by selecting "Multi-city" under Trip type in the advanced booking path. You can also click on this link to book multiple-stop trips: https://www.united.com/ual/en/US/flight-search/book-a-flight

Q. How many passengers can I book in a single flight reservation?

Ans. United allows booking of up to eight travelers at a time on united.com. To book air tickets for groups of nine travelers, Call +1-800-864-8331 within the U.S. and Canada or your local United customer service center. For groups of 10 or more, please get in touch with United Groups for special consideration.

Q. Can I make changes to my reservation after I purchase it?

Ans. Yes, United allows changes to most tickets, although a Basic Economy ticket cannot. If your ticket can be changed, you can do so in the 'Manage Reservations' section after purchasing. Keep in mind that a change fee may also apply.

Q. Which flights offer Basic Economy fares?

Ans. United Airlines offers Basic Economy fares on select routes. If available for a particular flight, the Basic Economy option will be displayed on the search results page on the official website and on the "Choose your travel experience" section of the official mobile app.

Q. How can I make changes to my name on the united.com account?

Ans. You can make changes to your name through the Personal Identification Change Request Form. Significant name changes will require you to submit identification or document verification. A simple name change, such as a spelling correction or addition or deletion of the middle name, will not require verification.

Select the "Profile & Preferences" section to make a simple name change and then select "Personal Info." You can also edit your information in the "About Me" section.

Q. How can I change my flight on the same day of travel for free?

Ans. Premier members can make changes on other flights for free when the originally purchased fare class is available on the new United Airlines flight. However, a difference in fare may apply even if the same original ticketed fare class is available for other customers.

Q. How do I contact a United Airlines customer care representative?

Ans. If any issue regarding United flight booking arises, you can contact a United customer care representative by dialing these numbers: +1-800-864-8331 or +1-888-814-6264. They are ready for your help 24*7.

Q. How do I know what I need for my trip with United?
